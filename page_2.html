Street Markings

These spray painted street markings can often be seen around Wellington. There appears to be a colour-coded system for gas, water, electricity, internet and telecommunications. Internet and telecommunication related markings are usually purple.

Chorus Marking

Presumably the Chor in this marking refers to the company Chorus.
