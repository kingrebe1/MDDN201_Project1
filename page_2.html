Street Markings

These spray painted street markings can often be seen around Wellington. There appears to be a colour-coded system for gas, water, electricity, internet and telecommunications. Internet and telecommunication related markings are usually purple.

Chorus Marking

Presumably the Chor in this marking refers to the company Chorus.

Unlabelled Marking

I could not find out what this marking means. It is similar to the previous marking, but lacks writing.

Unlabelled marking

Again, I couldn't find out what the marking meant. It is similar to the previous one, but without the top line.

Unlabelled Marking

This marking has even less detail still.

2XD Marking

Again the meaning is a mystery, but I noticed quite a few markings that had a number followed by XD.

No Chor

Again, I would assume the Chor here refers to Chorus.

3XD Marking

This marking is similar to many of the others, but has the number 3.

Chor F.O 10XD

Again it makes sense that Chor here refers to Chorus. It is likely that F.O stands for "Fibre Optic" referencing the fibre optic internet network that is being rolled out around the country.

Chorus Marking 1XC

Once again this probably refers to Chorus. This time the marking says XC instead of XD that we have seen in other examples.

Citylink Marking

Citylink markings seem to be far less common than Chorus ones. This marking was found in the Wellington CBD and probably has something to do with Citylink's fibre network. This marking was white rather than all the other markings, which were purple.
